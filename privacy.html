Privacy Policy

DN Splitter respects your privacy. This app is designed to help users split images and PDF pages into smaller parts.

1. Files you upload

Files selected inside the app are processed in the browser for preview and splitting. We do not intentionally collect or permanently store uploaded files on our servers unless a future online account or payment feature specifically says so.

2. Local storage

DN Splitter may store small settings in your browser, such as free usage count, unlock status, email entered for unlocking, and basic app preferences. This helps the app remember your state on your device.

3. Google sign-in

If Google sign-in is added later, we may store basic account information such as your name, email address, and profile picture only for account identification, unlock linking, and support.

4. Payments

If payment features are added later, payment processing may be handled by third-party payment providers. DN Splitter will not store sensitive card details directly on the app unless clearly stated.

5. Contact information

If you contact us for support, we may keep your email and message details to help solve your issue.

6. Changes

This privacy policy may be updated in the future as DN Splitter adds login, payment, and cloud features.

7. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact us through the Contact page.
